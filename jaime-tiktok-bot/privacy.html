This Privacy Policy explains how the TikTok Verification Bot ("the Bot"), operated by TPC Global LLC / Bold Evolution ("we", "us", "our"), collects, uses, and protects your information. Your privacy is important to us.

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Bot, we may collect the following information:

Data Type Purpose Retention
Discord User ID To track pending verifications and assign roles Temporary
TikTok Username To verify account ownership Temporary
Verification Code To match your TikTok bio with your Discord account Temporary

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Verification: Confirming you own the TikTok account you claim
  • Role Assignment: Granting the verified role upon successful verification
  • Service Operation: Ensuring the Bot functions correctly

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Store your TikTok bio content
  • Access your TikTok account or post on your behalf
  • Share your information with advertisers

3. Data Storage & Retention

Verification data (Discord ID, TikTok username, verification code) is stored temporarily in memory only during the verification process.

  • Data is automatically deleted after successful verification
  • Data is automatically deleted when the Bot restarts
  • No verification data is written to disk or persistent databases

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information:

  • Verification data is held in volatile memory only
  • Bot tokens and credentials are stored securely
  • We do not log sensitive user information

5. Third-Party Services

The Bot interacts with the following third-party services:

  • Discord: For bot functionality and user interaction. See Discord's Privacy Policy
  • TikTok: For fetching public profile information. See TikTok's Privacy Policy

We only access publicly available TikTok profile information (bio/signature).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Not Use the Bot: Verification is optional
  • Request Data Deletion: Contact server administrators to remove your verified role
  • Access Information: Ask what data, if any, is stored about you

7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.
