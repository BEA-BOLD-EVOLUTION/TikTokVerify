üìñ About

Learn more about the TikTok Verification Bot

What is this bot?

The TikTok Verification Bot is a Discord bot that enables communities to verify members' TikTok accounts. It bridges identities across platforms, allowing servers to recognize and connect with their TikTok audience.

Why verification?

Verification helps communities:

  • Know who's who between TikTok and Discord
  • Assign the correct roles to verified members
  • Build trust across platforms
  • Connect with their audience wherever they are

How does it work?

The bot generates a unique verification code that you temporarily add to your TikTok bio. Once the bot confirms the code is in your bio, you're verified! You can then remove the code.

Is it safe?

Absolutely! We never:

  • Ask for your TikTok password
  • Access your TikTok account
  • Post anything on your behalf
  • Store your data permanently

Who made this?

This bot is open source and available for any community to use.

üéØ How To Verify

Follow these simple steps to verify your TikTok account

Find the Verification Channel

In the Discord server, look for the verification channel where the bot has posted a verification panel.

Click "Verify TikTok"

Click the blue Verify TikTok button to start the verification process.

Enter Your TikTok Username

A popup will appear asking for your TikTok username. Enter it with or without the @ symbol.

Example: @yourname or yourname

Add the Code to Your Bio

The bot will give you a unique code like JAIME-12345. Open TikTok and add this code anywhere in your bio.

Click "I Added the Code"

Once the code is in your TikTok bio, click the green button. The bot will check your profile.

Get Verified! üéâ

If the code is found, you'll receive the Verified Viewer role instantly. You can now remove the code from your bio.

üí° Tip: Make sure your TikTok profile is set to public so the bot can read your bio!
